## Overview

The Reports Dashboard shows interactive charts and key metrics for work orders, revenue, and labor. Use the date range and branch/department filters to focus on the data you need.

## Filters

- **Date range** — Start and end dates (default: last 6 months)
- **Branch / Department** — Narrow results by branch and department
- **Refresh** — Reload the data

## KPI Cards

At the top of the dashboard, five summary cards show:

| KPI | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Invoices** | Number of invoices in the date range |
| **Revenue** | Total invoiced amount |
| **Open WOs** | Work orders opened in the period |
| **Closed WOs** | Work orders closed in the period |
| **Labor Hours** | Total billed labor hours |

## Charts

### Revenue by Month

Bar chart of total invoiced amount by invoice date. Shows how revenue trends over time.

### Work Orders Closed by Month

Bar chart of the count of closed work orders per month.

### Labor Hours Billed by Month

Bar chart of total billed labor hours per month.

### Revenue by Department

Pie chart showing invoice total by department. Helps compare revenue across departments.

### Open vs Closed by Department

Horizontal bar chart comparing open and closed work orders by department. Shows workload and completion by department.

## Tips

- Hover over chart elements to see detailed values
- Use a wider date range for longer-term trends
- Filter by branch/department to focus on a specific area
