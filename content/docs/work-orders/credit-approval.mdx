title: Credit Approval
description: Review and approve pending credit requests for customers over limit or with credit holds
---

## Overview

Credit Approval shows customers who are over their credit limit or have work orders with pending credit requests. You can review each customer's balance, expand to see their work orders, and approve credit where needed.

## Workflow

1. Open Credit Approval from the Work Orders page
2. Review the list of customers with pending credit review
3. Click a customer row to expand and see their work orders
4. For orders with **Pending approval**, click **Approve** to approve the credit
5. Click **View** to open the work order detail
6. Use **Refresh** to reload the list

## Customer Summary

Each customer row shows:

- **Customer** — Bill-to number and name
- **Balance** — Current balance (without credit)
- **Credit limit** — Customer's credit limit
- **Over limit** — If balance exceeds limit, shows how much over

### Badges

- **Over limit** — Customer's balance exceeds their credit limit
- **Credit requested** — One or more work orders have a pending credit request

## Work Order Details

When you expand a customer, you see their work orders:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| WO # | Work order number (click to open) |
| Status | Open, Closed, Quote, or Accepted |
| Credit | Pending approval or credit amount |
| Ship To | Ship-to location |
| Comments | Order notes |

### Actions

- **View** — Open the work order detail page
- **Approve** — Approve the credit request (only for orders pending approval)

## After Approving

When you approve a credit request, the work order is updated and the list refreshes. The order no longer appears as pending approval.
