## Overview

Reports provides Balance Sheet and Cash Flow statements as of a selected month-end date. Use the **Balance Sheet** tab to view assets and liabilities, and the **Cash Flow** tab to view sales, costs, expenses, gross margin, and income. Both reports support expandable sections to drill from high-level totals down to individual accounts. Filter by branch to focus on a specific location.

Data loads automatically when the page opens or when filters change. Click **Refresh** to reload.

---

## Tabs

| Tab | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Balance Sheet** | Assets and Liabilities as of the month-end date. Expandable by type, section, and account. |
| **Cash Flow** | Sales, costs, expenses, gross margin, and income. Expandable by activity and section. |

---

## Balance Sheet

The Balance Sheet shows financial position as of the last day of the selected month. Amounts combine debits and credits; negative amounts appear in parentheses.

### Structure

The report is organized in a hierarchy:

1. **Type** — Assets, Liabilities (top-level groups)
2. **Section** — Subgroups within each type (e.g., "Current Assets", "Fixed Assets")
3. **Account** — Individual account rows with Description and total

### Expandable Sections

- Click a **type** (Assets, Liabilities) to expand or collapse its sections.
- Click a **section** to expand or collapse its individual accounts.
- Chevron icons indicate state: down = expanded, right = collapsed.
- Totals appear next to each expandable row.

### Header

**Balance Sheet**  
*As of [Month] [Year]*

The "As of" date is the last day of the selected month (e.g., "As of March 31, 2025").

### Empty State

If no data exists for the period, the message "No balance sheet data for this period" appears.

## Cash Flow

The Cash Flow report shows sales, costs, expenses, and derived totals. It is organized into Operating Activities and Financial Activities.

### Operating Activities

| Section | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Sales** | Sales by section. Expand to see individual sales categories and amounts. |
| **Costs** | Cost of sales by section. Expand to see cost categories. |
| **Gross Margin** | Sales + Costs (typically costs are negative, so this is sales minus cost of goods sold). |

### Financial Activities

| Section | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Operating Expenses** | Expenses by section. Expand to see individual expense categories. |

### Totals (Bottom of Report)

| Line | Calculation |
|------|-------------|
| **Income Before Tax** | Sales + Costs + Expenses |
| **Net Cash for Period** | Same as Income Before Tax (simplified cash flow view) |

### Expandable Sections

- **Operating Activities** — Expands to show Sales, Costs, and Gross Margin.
- **Financial Activities** — Expands to show Operating Expenses.
- **Sales**, **Costs**, **Operating Expenses** — Each can be expanded to show section-level breakdown (e.g., "Parts", "Labor", "Rental").

### Header

**Cash Flow**  
*As of [Month] [Year]*

## Filters

### Branch

- **No branch selected** — All branches included.
- **Branch selected** — Only data for that branch. Uses the primary (first) selected branch if multiple are chosen.

The Branch filter applies to both Balance Sheet and Cash Flow.

### Month / Year

- **Month** — Dropdown (January–December). Select the month for the report.
- **Year** — Dropdown (current year ± 3). Select the year.
- **As-of date** — The report uses the **last day** of the selected month (e.g., March 31 for March).
- **Default** — Current month and current year.

Changing month or year reloads the data.

---

## Refresh

Click **Refresh** to reload Balance Sheet and Cash Flow data from the server. Use after changing filters or if data may have been updated elsewhere.

---

## Amount Formatting

- Positive amounts: `$1,234.56`
- Negative amounts: `($1,234.56)` — parentheses indicate a credit or negative balance

---

## Use Cases

- **Month-end close** — Run the Balance Sheet and Cash Flow as of month-end to verify totals before closing.
- **Branch review** — Filter by branch to see financial position and cash flow for a specific location.
- **Historical comparison** — Select prior months to compare assets, liabilities, sales, and expenses over time.
- **Drill-down** — Expand sections to trace totals to individual accounts or categories.
- **Audit support** — Use the expandable structure to verify that high-level totals roll up correctly from detail.
