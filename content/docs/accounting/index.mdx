## Overview

The Accounting module is the central hub for financial data, reports, and general ledger management. From the main page, you can access summary charts, the chart of accounts, budget tracking, financial reports, manual journal entries, equipment and customer ledgers, and AR history.

## What You Can Do

- [Summary Charts](/docs/accounting/dashboard) — View sales, expenses, and overdue trends
- [Chart of Accounts](/docs/accounting/chart) — Browse accounts with MTD/YTD totals
- [Budget](/docs/accounting/budget) — Track department performance and set budgets
- [Reports](/docs/accounting/reports) — Balance Sheet and Cash Flow statements
- [Manual Journal](/docs/accounting/journal) — Record adjustments and corrections in the GL
- [Equipment Ledger](/docs/accounting/equipment) — View GL entries by equipment
- [Customer AR](/docs/accounting/customer) — Open invoices and AR activity by customer
- [AR History](/docs/accounting/ar-history) — Search AR records
- [WIP & Daily Reports](/docs/accounting/operations-reports) — WIP summary and daily sales

## Main Page

The Accounting main page displays **Quick Actions** — cards that link to each submodule. Click any card to open that area. Use the Branch/Department filter where available to focus on specific locations.
