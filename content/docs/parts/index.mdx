## Overview

The Parts Manager module is the central hub for searching parts, managing inventory, approving work order parts requests, receiving purchase orders, and working with assemblies (BOMs). From the main page, you can search parts directly and use Quick Actions to navigate to KPI dashboards, approval queues, inventory tables, warehouse search, transfers, bin scanning, counts, assemblies, and price uploads.

## What You Can Do

- [Parts KPI Dashboard](/docs/parts/kpi) — Fill rate, inventory turns, stock-to-critical, and inventory reports
- [Parts Approval](/docs/parts/approval) — Approve or deny work order parts requests by work order
- [Inventory Tables](/docs/parts/inventory-tables) — Backorders, requisitions, stale parts, and consignments
- [Warehouse Search](/docs/parts/warehouse) — Search parts by warehouse or across all warehouses
- [Part Detail](/docs/parts/part-detail) — Detail page for a part in a specific warehouse
- [Receive Orders](/docs/parts/receive) — Receive parts from purchase orders
- [Transfer](/docs/parts/transfer) — Move parts between warehouses
- [Scan Parts / Bin](/docs/parts/scan) — Lookup bin contents via manual entry or camera scan
- [Parts Count](/docs/parts/count) — Create and work inventory counts
- [Assembly (BOM)](/docs/parts/assembly) — Create and manage parts assemblies
- [Upload Prices](/docs/parts/upload) — Upload manufacturer price files and optionally reprice parts

## Main Page

On `/parts`, you get:

- **Search Parts** — A search box that uses the Parts search API. Type at least 3 characters to search by part number, description, or vendor. Selecting a result navigates to the Part Detail page for that part and warehouse.
- **Quick Actions** — Cards for KPI Dashboard, Approval, Inventory Tables, Transfer, Receive, Count, Assembly, Warehouse, Upload Prices, and Scan. Each card links to the corresponding Parts submodule.
